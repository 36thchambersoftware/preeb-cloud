Who We Are

Built for Decentralization

PREEB is a community-first Cardano stake pool run by a single operator that believes decentralization is the backbone of a healthy blockchain ecosystem. Large centralized pools undermine the very principles that make Cardano special — we exist to be part of the solution.

As a small pool operator, every ADA you delegate is meaningful. Your support directly helps the pool produce blocks, deliver consistent rewards, and prove that independent pools can thrive. The Cardano network is strongest when stake is spread across many operators — not concentrated in a handful of mega-pools.

PREEB wants to keep delegators earning. PREEB is committed to low fees, reliable infrastructure, and transparent communication so your ADA always works hard for you.

Why It Matters

Support Small Pools

Cardano's Ouroboros protocol is designed for thousands of independent pools to participate. When delegators flood into saturated mega-pools, rewards drop and decentralization suffers. By choosing PREEB you're casting a vote for a healthier, more distributed Cardano.

  • Smaller pools earn the same rewards per block as big pools
  • Under-saturated pools have room for more rewards
  • More pools = more censorship resistance
  • Your delegation directly funds independent operation

Get Started

How to Delegate to PREEB

Delegating is non-custodial — your ADA never leaves your wallet. You can re-delegate or withdraw at any time.

1

Get a Cardano Wallet

Download a Cardano light wallet such as Eternl. Create a wallet and fund it with ADA.

2

Open the Staking Dashboard

In your wallet, navigate to the Staking or Delegation section. This is where you choose which pool to support.

3

Search for PREEB

Type PREEB in the pool search box. Select the PREEB Stake Pool from the results and confirm.

4

Confirm & Earn

Pay the small one-time transaction fee and sign the delegation transaction. Rewards start after the current epoch ends (~5 days).
